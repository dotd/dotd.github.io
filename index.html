About Us

What is Bosch Center for AI?

The Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence (BCAI) was created in 2017 out of existing competence centers to deploy cutting-edge AI technologies across Bosch products and services creating solutions that are "Invented for life". We employ roughly 270 associates worldwide, dedicated to over 185 projects within seven locations: Germany, India, United States, Israel and China.

Bosch-AI in Haifa

Bosch Center for AI in Haifa was founded in January 1st 2019. It main focus is Computer Vision based Reinforcement Learning. It is located on the Beach of Haifa in close vicinity to the Technion, Israel Institute of Technology amd the Haifa University.

What We Do?

Our everyday work is composed of 3 parts:
(1) Writing papers for leading AI and robotics conferences such as NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ICCV, ECCV, CVPR, IROS, ICRA, RSS, etc. that are at the core of our skills.
(2) Working with Business Units (BUs) in order to solve hard problems and develop new methodologies.
(3) Protecting our work with patents so we can publish our work.
